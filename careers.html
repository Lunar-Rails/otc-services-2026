Culture

Working at OTC Services

At OTC Services, we foster a relaxed work culture, free from corporate hierarchy and with a genuine focus on work-life balance. Team members enjoy autonomy and take personal ownership of their work, making a tangible impact on the company and driving it forward.

We prioritize connection and community, regardless of location, and believe that in-person communication, collaboration, and creativity are irreplaceable. When not building blockchain infrastructure, our team enjoys coffee, team events, industry discussions, and keeping a close eye on market trends.
